📚 How to Use This Scanner

📷 Copy Device ID Button

After scanning a QR code, click this button to copy the device ID to your clipboard. You can then paste it anywhere you need the device information. The button becomes active only after a successful scan.

✅ Add to Account Button

Clicking this button will take you to your account management page where you can officially register this device. This is the main action you'll perform after scanning. You'll be redirected to complete the device setup.

🔄 Reset Scanner Button

Use this button to clear the current scan and start fresh. This resets the scanner display and allows you to scan a new device. Useful if you made a mistake or want to scan multiple devices.

💡 Tips for Best Results

Ensure good lighting for faster scans. Hold the device steady at a slight angle to the QR code. Keep the code within the camera frame. If the scan is slow, try moving closer to the QR code or adjusting the angle.
